The information presented here is from a research study undertaken by oioitoi during March–April 2026, as part of Startup Virginia's 'Idea Factory' customer discovery process. A number of parents were interviewed to gain insight into how they play with their young children and how they prepare them with the skills needed to start Kindergarten.

The interview process identified several areas of concern, which were then put to a wider audience for validation as part of a survey — the results of which appear below.

We would like to acknowledge the participants for so graciously offering their time and recounting their honest experiences. We really appreciate you. Thank you!

oioitoi is a play-focused school readiness startup, whose aim is to take the pain out of parenting.
